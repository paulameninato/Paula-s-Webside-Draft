Blueprint for
Creative Dissent

Are you passionate about social change?

Are you sick of wondering if your endless marching will generate change?

Do you want to be more creative in your activism?

If you answered “yes” to these questions, I know exactly how you feel: I was there myself. During my high school and college years, I was extremely passionate about social change but I didn't achieve much despite going to countless marches because I didn't know what I was doing.

After organizing for over ten years and reading extensively about creative activism and politial organizing, I wrote Blueprint for Creative Dissent. I want you to avoid making the same mistakes I made, so I'm giving you the eBook for free! All you have to do is click the link below.

Download Your FREE eBook
