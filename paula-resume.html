Paula Meninato

Artist & Activist

I'm an artist engaged in practice-led research on how art can be used to dismantle cognitive barriers that enable systemic oppression. I apply concepts learned from academic research on political organizing, creative activism and the psychology of oppresive ideologies to my art. I then use my art as a tool start conversations about social justice issues and political organizing.

Freelance Web Designer

Web Design for Artists

May 2018 - Present

Due to my success in the promotion of my art, other artists have hired me to help them improve their promotional techniques and marketing materials. A website is arguably one of the most crucial promotional tools for artists, yet most artists miss this opporunity because they don't actually know web design. Therefore, I launched Web Design for Artists to design gorgeous websites for artists that present their work in a professional manner to help them land new opportunities.

My qualifications:

  • Extensive curitorial experience, including selecting artists for exhibitions at Philadelphia City Hall and The School District of Philadelphia
  • Art sales experience, including fundraising over $3,000 for the Humane League from art sales
  • Proficient in HTML, CSS, and Wordpress
