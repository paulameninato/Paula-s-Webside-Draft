Paula Meninato

Artist & Activist

I'm an artist engaged in practice-led research on how art can be used to dismantle cognitive barriers that enable systemic oppression. I apply concepts learned from academic research on political organizing, creative activism and the psychology of oppresive ideologies to my art. I then use my art as a tool start conversations about social justie issues and political organizing. Finally, I give workshops to help passionate indivuals create meaningful change in their communities.
